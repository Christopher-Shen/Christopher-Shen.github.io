COVID-19 Entertainmnent

Listed below are some information and rules concerning how this community will operate. If you have any questions, please reach out to Chris directly.

  1. You must pre-deposit with your respective banker. Chris for Columbia and Brandon for Penn.
  2. You may cash out at most 1 time per rolling week. This is to ensure flow isn't terrible for bankes.
  3. You must cash out a minimum of $200 worth of your deposit. This is to mitigate the number of transactions.
  4. To cash out, directly message your respective banker notifiying them of your request. Please give them 2-3 days to fulfill your request.
  5. You may request an update on how much you have on deposit at any time, but please allow some time for the bankers to respond.
  6. If you have concerns about the liquidity of either of the two bankers, please bring it to the attention of the whole group.
  7. Balances will be settled after each game and will be reflected immediately (you can ask your banker directly after a game has concluded.
  8. It costs in game coins to run a game, the cost of these coins will be split proportinally based on the number of hands played in each session.
  9. Coins will be calcualted on a per dollar basis and the number of coins used in a game will inform how much total rake will be taken from the game.
